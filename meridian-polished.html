Our residential brand

The Vantage collection.

Vantage is Meridian's signature residential brand — a growing collection of design-forward communities built in the region's most sought-after transit neighborhoods. Each property is shaped by its location and backed by our full in-house platform: thoughtfully designed, expertly built, and managed with an owner's accountability. Vantage sets a new bar for what renters can expect from a neighborhood and a home.
